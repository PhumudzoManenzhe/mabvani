About UniApply SA

One Application Journey. Multiple Universities.

UniApply SA simplifies the South African university application process by allowing students to create one profile, upload documents once, receive programme recommendations, and track all applications in a single platform.

Get Started Explore Features

Our Mission

To simplify university applications across South Africa by creating a single, transparent and student-friendly platform.

Our Vision

To become South Africa's trusted university application hub, helping students make informed educational decisions.

Why UniApply SA?

Repeated Information

Students enter the same details on multiple university websites.

Different APS Rules

Universities calculate admission scores differently.

Missed Deadlines

Important dates and document requirements are often overlooked.

Application Tracking

Tracking applications across many portals can be difficult.
